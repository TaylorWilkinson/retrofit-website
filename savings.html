Who doesn’t love saving money? Retrofitting can save you hundreds of dollars on your energy bills each month. Old and inefficient are creating extra expenditures that could be solved by updating your home’s energy systems. Find out more below.

The average retrofitted home saw an increase of about $23/month on their electricity bills. Even though there is a small increase in electricity retrofit, the overall efficiency will increase significantly and reduce emissions and benefit the environment.

The average retrofitted home saw a decrease of about $887/month on their natural gas bills. That’s a lot of extra heat and warm meals for the same price. This is definitely going to be important for keeping warm during the winter months.

The average retrofitted home saw an decrease of $864/month monthly on their combined energy bill. In addition to saving nearly a thousand dollars a month, a more energy efficient will have reduced emissions and overall environmental impact.
