In addition to saving you moneyand saving the environment. Retrofitting can also save your health! Find out how updating your furnace can improve air quality in your home and save you from illnesses! Who even has time to be sick these days?

Some furnaces that may help you update your system are:
Baseboard Furnace: more silent and requires less ongoing maintenace to a forced air furnace
Forced Air Furnace: these heat the home fairly quickly, however they do require frequent regular maintenance
Condensing Furnace: large and require annual maintenance, but will result in low long term energy cost

Instantaneous (Pilot): Flame is lit with a pilot
Instantaneous (Condensing): Tankless Water Heaters from Rheem provide a continuous supply of hot water from an energy-efficient and space-saving design
Conserver Tank: Low initial cost, energy efficient however energy is used throughout the day to maintain water temperature.

Oil: Systems with this fuel are harder to obtain due to the limited fuel source, however they are very reliable
Gas: This fuel provides more hot water and reheats faster than electric water systems
Electricity: Systems with this fuel are easier to maintain and generally cleaner (as no fuel lines or venting is required)
