In addition to saving you moneyand saving theenvironment, retrofitting can also save your health! Find out how updating your furnace can improve air quality in your home and save you from illnesses! Who even has time to be sick these days?

Air quality in your home is the key to keeping yourself and those you live with healthy. In order to maintain clean air in your home it is important to make sure your furnace ( the main air filter in your home) is updated!

People who have air-sensitive illnesses such as asthma are especially vulnerable to being triggered by poor air quality in their homes. The unfiltered dust and other allergens can aggravate allergies and decrease the overall quality of life in your home.

Older outdated furnaces filter poorly but also inadequately attempt to warm homes. People who live in colder homes have a higher chance of catching respiratory illnesses such as the flu. Updating your home’s furnace can help you keep from getting sick and keep your cozy.
